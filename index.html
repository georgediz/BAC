About Us

Birdtown Athletics Club is a non-profit organization dedicated to nurturing high school runners, college-level athletes, and top-tier competitors. Our mission is to provide unparalleled coaching, personal training, and a vibrant community that cultivates growth and success.

With our experienced coaches and runners, we offer personal training that addresses each athlete's unique strengths and weaknesses. Our club's training sessions take place at different times throughout the year, providing a convenient and motivating environment for all individuals.

Training

At Birdtown Athletics Club, we offer a range of comprehensive training designed to improve endurance, speed, and overall performance. Our programs include:

  • High-Intensity Interval Training
  • Long Runs
  • Strength and Conditioning
  • Injury Prevention
  • Easy Runs
  • Aerobic Focus

Whether you're a high school runner aiming for collegiate success, an elite athlete seeking to reach new heights, or an individual who just wants to push their limits, our training is tailored to meet your specific needs and goals.
